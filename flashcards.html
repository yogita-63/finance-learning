FINANCE

Its a management of money and includes activities such as investing, borrowing, lending, budgeting, saving, and forecasting

BITCOIN

a digital and global money system currency which allows people to send or receive money across the internet

SAVING

the amount left over after an individual's consumer spending is subtracted from the amount of disposable income earned in a given period of time.

LOAN

A loan is when you receive money from a friend, bank or financial institution in exchange for future repayment of the principal and interest.

INVESTING

Investing means that an asset is bought,
or that money is put into a bank to get a future interest from it.
